

 Employee Elevate - HR Assistant Chatbot Responses

 Introduction
Hello! I'm your HR assistant. I can help you with questions about leave policies, performance reviews, company benefits, and more. How can I assist you today?

 Leave Policies
Q: How many leave days am I entitled to?  
A: Employees are entitled to 15 paid leave days per year, which can be used for vacation, personal time, or sick leave.

Q: How do I apply for leave? 
A: To apply for leave, please visit the Leave section on the Employee Portal and click on "Apply for Leave." Ensure to submit your application at least two weeks in advance.

Q: What happens if I exceed my leave days? 
A: Exceeding your leave days without prior approval may lead to a deduction from your salary or disciplinary action. Please manage your leave balance accordingly.

 Performance Reviews
Q: When are performance reviews conducted?  
A: Performance reviews are conducted quarterly. You will be notified in advance regarding the review process.

Q: How is my performance evaluated?  
A: Performance is evaluated based on the number of projects and tasks completed, as well as the total hours worked.

Q: Can I request feedback outside of the review period?  
A: Yes, you can request informal feedback from your manager at any time. Regular check-ins are encouraged for continuous improvement.

Company Benefits
Q: What benefits does the company offer? 
A: Our company offers a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement plans, paid time off, and professional development opportunities.

Q: How do I enroll in the health insurance plan?  
A: You can enroll in the health insurance plan during the open enrollment period. Please visit the Employee Portal for more details.

Q: Are there any employee discounts available?  
A: Yes, employees are eligible for discounts on various services and products. Check the Employee Portal for a list of current discounts.

General Inquiries
